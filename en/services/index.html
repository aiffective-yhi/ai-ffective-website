Field 1

Identify AI potential

Before we build, we establish what is worth building. These services deliver the foundation: a target picture, your real workflows, and an operating model that fits your company.

01

AI Ambition & Target Picture

Assessment · Phase 1

Where does AI pay off in your company — and where doesn't it? We evaluate your business areas by effort, benefit, and feasibility, and define with you which level you are aiming for: individual relief points, end-to-end workflows, or the intelligence layer across your entire operations.

OutcomeA prioritized target picture with the 3 biggest levers and an ROI estimate per lever.
02

Workflow Analysis & Redesign

Phase 1

In interviews with your key people, we capture the real workflows — not the documented ones. Then we redesign them: which steps does AI take over, which stay with people, and where does coordination disappear entirely?

OutcomeRedesigned workflows for your most important processes — including 2–3 quick wins you can implement immediately.
03

AI Operating Model

Phase 1

For AI to hold up in daily operations, it needs a place in your organization: who is responsible for what, which data may go where, cloud or fully local? We define the operating model before anything gets built.

OutcomeAn action plan with roles, data access, and the track decision (cloud or local) — the blueprint for Phase 2.
Field 2

Build & operate the solution

The target picture becomes a running system. These services build your company's intelligence layer — and keep it current in operations.

04

Company Memory

Phase 2

The core of every solution: your strategy, organization, projects, and decision history — captured in a structured way, so AI doesn't answer generically but understands your operations. This is the part no off-the-shelf tool brings with it.

OutcomeA structured knowledge base that gives every AI tool full company context. The data belongs to you.
05

AI Workflows & Briefings

Phase 2

We design how your leadership works with the system: the daily briefing at 8 a.m., decision documents in your management board's format, reports that arrive before anyone asks. No new tool to learn — the information comes to you.

OutcomeLeadership briefings and templates that work in your daily routine from day one.
06

Agentic Architectures

Phase 2

For recurring tasks, we build agents that work independently: compiling reports, monitoring delivery dates, escalating deviations. Every agent works on your company memory — within clearly defined boundaries.

OutcomeAutomations that take over routine work without anyone having to trigger them.
07

Development & Data Integration

Phase 2

We set up the AI system and connect your sources: ERP, CRM, finance, project management. In two tracks — cloud for maximum capability, or fully local when no data may leave your premises.

OutcomeA working system on your real data — first briefings are live before the build phase ends.
08

Testing & Validation

Phase 2 · Phase 3

Before a briefing or agent goes live, we test it against real cases from your operations. After that, we monitor quality continuously and correct when answers drift from reality — before anyone notices.

OutcomeReliable output your leadership can trust — permanently, not just at launch.
09

Embedding & Adoption

Phase 2 · Phase 3

A system only helps if it gets used. We onboard your leaders personally, expand usage step by step to new areas and people, and adapt the system when your company changes.

OutcomeA system that grows with your company — instead of a project that gathers dust after handover.
10

Safety & Guardrails

Phase 2 · Phase 3

We define what the system may do — and what it may not: access rights per person, clear boundaries for agents, handling of sensitive data. FADP- and GDPR-compliant, fully local with open-source models on request.

OutcomeA system your management board can approve with a clear conscience — with data sovereignty from day one.
